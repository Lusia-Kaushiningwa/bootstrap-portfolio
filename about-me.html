Who Am I?

I am Lusia. A passionate beginner full-stack developer.

I am a curious and driven developer who enjoys turning ideas into clean, functional, and visually appealing web experiences. My journey into tech started with a simple curiosity about how websites work, and quickly turned into a passion for building them myself.

Growth happens when curiosity meets consistency.

I enjoy solving problems, learning new technologies, and constantly improving my skills. Every project I build represents progress — not just in coding, but in creativity, structure, and confidence.

How it all started...

My journey into development began when I decided to understand what happens "behind the screen." What started as simple HTML experiments quickly grew into learning CSS layouts, JavaScript logic, and building full interactive pages from scratch.

- It started with curiosity and a lot of trial and error.

In the beginning, I faced challenges understanding positioning, responsiveness, and JavaScript logic. But every bug I solved strengthened my problem-solving skills and taught me patience.

That mindset is what truly shaped me as a developer.
